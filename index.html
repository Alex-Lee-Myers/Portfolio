About Me

Hello world! I'm an Indiana-based full-stack web developer who comes molded from background experiences involving time-intensive problem solving, vigilant attention to detail, and constant collaboration.

Current Position

Presently, I work full-time as the Information Technology Coordinator at Indiana University. My day-to-day requires active technical troubleshooting, constant supervision of up to 7 consultants at any given time, and managing an ecosystem of over 2,000 devices.

Future Prospects

My next goal in my career is to become a full-time web developer. I truly believe my past career experiences gave me a baseline of skills that directly translate into being a fast-learner when it comes to this field.

Project Portfolio

Here you can see my web development projects, ranging from incorporating API calls in a website, utilizing React in a small development group, and designing this Portfolio page via Material Design Bootstrap. Complimenting this is experiences in design, technical documentation creation, and mentoring consultants at my current position. Additionally, my skills as a leader and technical expert are frequently put to the test in crunch-time situations when managing a team that delivers multi-day events to over 500 in-person audience members and tens of thousands of virtual participants.
